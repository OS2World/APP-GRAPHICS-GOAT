Notes on JPEG support:  JPEG is not currently supported for several reasons.  First, it is a lossy format which is particularly ill suited to OCR applications.  Second, the current legal fight surrounding the format and patent claims by Forgent Networks and the possible licensing issues which may ensue.  Finally, the possible withdrawl of the ISO/IEC 10918-1 standard.  Until the resolution of these issues becomes more clear, JPEG support is unlikely.
